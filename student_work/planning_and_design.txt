=========================
APP PLANNING & DESIGN
========================= 

1. PROJECT NAME & THEME
-------------------------
- What is your app called, and what type of app is it?  
"Account Roulette"
- What style or vibe do you want users to experience?
I want the users to experience the thrill of gambling with their accounts. (Completely free!)
- What about your app will require a log-in structure?
To spin the roulette wheel, you must create an account and log-in.

2. CORE FUNCTIONALITY
-------------------------
- What are the essential features your app must have?
A spinning roulette wheel that bans accounts when players lose.
- What optional features could make your app more interactive or personalized?
Customizable roulette wheel and prizes for surviving on the wheel.

3. SECRET OR PERSONALIZED CONTENT
Admin can see all accounts and ban those who are winning too much?
-------------------------
- What content will users see after logging in?  
A roulette wheel and an explanation of the app.
- Will this content change depending on who is using the app (admin, user, special user?)
Admins and owners have other options besides spinning the wheel, like banning players.

4. USER EXPERIENCE (UX) DESIGN
-------------------------
- How should your app look and feel?
It should feel like a dark and dingy casino.
- How will users navigate between pages?
There aren't any pages to navigate. Only a roulette wheel, unless they are an admin.
Admins will have buttons that normal users do not have, like viewing a list of users.

5. SECURITY FEATURES
-------------------------
- What password and authentication rules will you enforce?  
Require basic password complexity, and check security level.
- How will you protect secret pages from unauthorized access?
When a admin or special user logs in, they will be given access to the secret pages. Basic users will be kicked out if they try to get in.

6. OPTIONAL CREATIVE ENHANCEMENTS
-------------------------
- What customization or fun features could users enjoy?
Cool ASCII art, users may be able to change wheel color.
- Are there interactive elements, like games, quizzes, or badges, you could include?
We could include other gambling games other than roulette.
